What We're Up To ?

We’re excited to share that Bi Motors now offers high-quality Paint Protection Film (PPF) services. This premium transparent film provides a strong, durable shield against scratches, stone chips, road debris, and everyday wear. With exceptional clarity and long-lasting protection, PPF helps preserve your vehicle’s original paint while keeping it looking fresh and new. It’s the perfect choice for drivers who want maximum protection without compromising the car’s appearance.

Our shop has recently introduced a full Nano Polish treatment combined with high-quality Ceramic Coating to deliver a deeper, longer-lasting shine for every vehicle. This upgrade enhances paint clarity, restores smoothness, and provides a protective ceramic layer that shields against UV rays, dirt, chemicals, and everyday wear. With improved gloss, easier maintenance, and extended durability, this service is designed to keep your car looking newer for much longer—reflecting the same care and qualityBi Motors is known for.
